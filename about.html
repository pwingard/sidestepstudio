Sidestep Studio

Sidestep Studio is a small independent studio building astrophotography software and telling the story of the night sky. Our apps — Moondance, Astro Dust Up, FOV Planner and more — help imagers plan, capture and understand what they shoot.

The imaging and the videos go out under See the Show — a running series of deep-sky captures and short films made from a backyard under dark skies in Camden, South Carolina.

Follow along on YouTube and Instagram.
