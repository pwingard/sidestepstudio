Moondance

Methodical Imaging Planner

Plan astrophotography sessions around the moon, your targets, and your sky. See a full year of imaging windows at a glance.

Free Beta in TestFlight

Collect your DSOs. Image them methodically.

Most astronomy apps show you the sky. Moondance shows you when to image it.

SkySafari, Stellarium, and the rest are beautiful sky maps — they render constellations, planets, and deep-sky objects in real time. Perfect for visual observers, navigation, education.

But if you're an imager, you're not asking "what's in the sky right now." You're asking:

  • Which of my targets are imageable this week, or this month?
  • When is the moon out of the way of them?
  • Can I fit M51 and NGC 4236 in tonight, given when each clears my horizon?
  • I've shot 53 Messier objects — which is the easiest next one this month?

Atlas tools don't really answer those questions. They give you the sky; they don't give you a plan.

Moondance is built for imagers and collectors — people who want to capture their targets methodically, over time. It answers "what should I shoot, given what I want and what the sky's doing?"

With my Origin telescope I just imaged a dark nebula in Atlanta — under a full moon, in the city glow. Moondance found the targets minimally affected by the moonlight… and it actually worked.

Key Features

Collections (with thumbnails) let you group and save targets by constellation, object type, season, equipment, or any criteria you choose. Once saved, you can easily pull from these collections to build a target list for any given night.

Night Scrubber — Drag the clock hand around the sky dome to sweep through the night from sunset to sunrise. Watch constellations rise and set in real time. Use it to scout what's available at specific hours or to catch targets that are only up for a short window.

Smart Moon Handling — Moondance is built around the idea that the Moon's phase and position should not automatically rule out imaging. With adjustable moon tolerance settings, you can identify usable imaging time based on your equipment, abilities, and preferences.

Search over 2,000 deep sky objects, with a Messier tracker built in to record progress on all 108.

All the revolving data for DSOs in constant slow motion, richly complex yet simplistically beautiful, is pulled together and presented in a clear graphical interface.

With Moondance, plan methodically and image with intention.
