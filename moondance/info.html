Moondance

Build Collections · Maximize Darkness · Image Methodically

Moondance is a data-driven astrophotography planner designed by an astrophotographer for those who want a more methodical approach to imaging the night sky.

The app allows you to explore any night of the year for suitable targets — and any target for its best available nights throughout the year.

Key Features

Collections (with thumbnails) let you group and save targets by constellation, object type, season, equipment, or any criteria you choose. Once saved, you can easily pull from these collections to build a target list for any given night.

Night Scrubber — Drag the clock hand around the sky dome to sweep through the night from sunset to sunrise. Watch constellations rise and set in real time. Use it to scout what's available at specific hours or to catch targets that are only up for a short window.

Smart Moon Handling — Moondance is built around the idea that the Moon's phase and position should not automatically rule out imaging. With adjustable moon tolerance settings, you can identify usable imaging time based on your equipment, abilities, and preferences.

Search over 2,000 deep sky objects.

All the revolving data for DSOs in constant slow motion, richly complex yet simplistically beautiful, is pulled together and presented in a clear graphical interface.

…and much more.

With Moondance, plan methodically and image with intention.
