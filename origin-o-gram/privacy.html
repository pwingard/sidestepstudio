origin-o-gram — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

The app itself collects nothing

origin-o-gram runs entirely on your Mac. It connects only to your own Celestron Origin over your local network and reads files you point it at. It has no analytics, no telemetry, no user accounts, and sends none of your images or data anywhere.

The download form collects your email

When you download from this website, we ask for your email address, which we store with our email provider (Kit / ConvertKit) to send you your download link and occasional updates about Sidestep Studio apps. We do not sell or share it. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email. Email handling is subject to Kit's own privacy and anti-spam compliance.
